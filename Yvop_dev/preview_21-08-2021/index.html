About Us

Voices On Paper met in August 2020 to help each other improve their scripts, which went on to become finalists in writing competitions. The chemistry and fun together was so natural, that VOP expanded organically to create a weekly table read workshop group to help all writers.

Forming a team of script doctors and performers with a progressive mindset, VOP aims to deliver high quality storytelling, with members studied in various acting methods, and who earned numerous awards for writing, directing, acting, and producing.

Voices on Paper provides a safe space for creatives to share their work, hear their concepts and dialogue out loud, and receive constructive feedback through a diverse set of lenses.

Our mission is to foster collaborative minds and talents together to revise good scripts into great scripts, and great scripts into exceptional ones.

The Team

The team ranges wide in ethnic diversity, ages, LGBTQAI+ and allies, fluency in foreign languages, talent for accents, dialects, and voices, and freedom with gender bending roles.

The Lab

Pilots, episodic scripts, features, and short films. Any genre.

Process:

We start the meeting with introductions, read your script, and give feedback on story and format. Then the floor is yours to ask additional questions about your work.

All done via Zoom.

Submission Requirements

ALL SCRIPT MUST BE REGISTERED OR HAVE A COPYRIGHT.

  • Title
  • Genre
  • Number of pages

Submit to voicesonpaper@yourvoicesonpaper.com

  • Logline (optional)
  • Tagline (optional)
  • Writer’s notes (optional)
  • List of Speaking roles with character descriptions
  • Copy of the script
  • Who referred you to VOP?
